Things to remember before doing meditation


  • Prepare your space.
  • Prepare your body.
  • Remove distractions.
  • Release tension.
  • Set your seat.
  • Gather your intentions.
  • Fully commit to your practice and decide on the length of time.
  • Set a timer.
  • Refine your posture.
  • Adjust your breath.
